## Overview

In this age of prevalent AI-generated content, the importance of **human-created content** has become paramount. That's why I've developed a **static website** with near-perfect page load speeds, dedicated solely to delivering content.

## Features

### Performance & Reach

* **Blazing Fast**: Enjoy **near-instant page loads** for an exceptional user experience and reduced bounce rates.
* **Highly Responsive**: Content looks great on **any device**, adapting seamlessly to all screen sizes.
* **SEO-Optimized**: Designed to be **easily discoverable**, helping your content reach a wider audience.

### Streamlined Content Creation

* **Markdown Support**: Write effortlessly in Markdown, which **automatically converts to HTML**, letting you focus on content over formatting.
* **Syntax Highlighting**: Beautiful and accurate **Shiki highlighting** ensures code snippets are clear and visually appealing. Choose from various themes to match your style.
